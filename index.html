COME AS YOU ARE

You belong here before you believe anything.

We're not a program. We're not a lecture. We're a room full of real people sharing real food and having real conversations about faith, life, and what it means to follow Jesus together.

Whether you're brand-new to the Bible or have been following Jesus for years, you'll find genuine community here. No perfect answers required. No dress code. Just show up, grab some food, and jump into the conversation.

This is a Grace Family Church community — and we're building something special in St. Pete.

YOUR FIRST NIGHT

Here's what happens on a Thursday

No surprises. Just good people, good food, and good conversation.

6:30

Show Up & Eat

Food is ready when you walk in. Grab a plate, sit down, and meet the people around you. Music's playing. It's relaxed.

6:50

A Short Teaching

Clear, simple, and applicable to real life. Not a sermon — more like a conversation starter for the room.

7:05

Table Discussion

This is the heart of the night. Small group conversations around questions that actually matter. Share what you want — no one's putting you on the spot.

7:45

Next Steps

A simple invitation to go deeper — whether that's joining a smaller group, serving, or just coming back next Thursday.
